Frequently Asked Questions

🍰 Do you offer vegan or gluten-free options?

Absolutely! We have a range of vegan and gluten-free treats. Just ask our team when ordering!

📶 Do you have Wi-Fi for customers?

Yes, free Wi-Fi is available. Ask any barista for the password and enjoy browsing with your brew.

🐶 Is Pink Café pet-friendly?

Yes! Leashed pets are welcome in our outdoor areas. Pup cups are available for your furry friends.

🎉 Can I book a space for private events or birthdays?

Of course! Our café can be reserved for special occasions. Visit our Events page for booking details.

💳 What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept all major cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash.

📦 Do you offer delivery or pickup?

Yes! Order through delivery apps or directly from our Shop page for pickup orders.

☕ What are your café hours?

We’re open Monday–Friday: 7:30 AM – 7:00 PM, and Saturday–Sunday: 8:00 AM – 8:00 PM.

🌸 Do you use organic or locally sourced ingredients?

Yes! We prioritize organic and local products to ensure quality and support our community.

🎨 Do you host workshops or creative events?

We love hosting creative events! Check our Events page for upcoming sessions.

🥤 Can I customize my drink or pastry?

Yes! We’re happy to tailor your order — just let us know your preferences.
