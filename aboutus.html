Our Story

Welcome to Pink Cafe, where every detail from the pastel decor to the lovingly crafted drinks is designed to spark joy. Founded in 2022 by three life long friends who shared a passion for art, coffee, and community, our cozy corner of the world quickly became a gathering spot for dreamers, creatives, and coffee aficionados alike. We believe that a single cup can do more than warm your hands it can bring people together, ignite conversations, and inspire new ideas.

Over the years, we've curated a menu that celebrates local ingredients, seasonal flavors, and a touch of whimsy. Our baristas are trained not only to pour the perfect latte but also to craft each cup with a sprinkle of love whether that's in the form of delicate latte art or a dash of rose syrup. Every pastry in our display case is baked fresh daily by our in-house pastry chef, who draws inspiration from classic recipes and modern twists.

At Pink Cafe, you'll often find live acoustic evenings, watercolor workshops, and open mic poetry nights. We believe in nurturing our community, which is why we host monthly ''coffee & canvas'' sessions where local artists can showcase their work. Our walls rotate with new paintings, photographs, and installations so that every visit feels like discovering a hidden gem.

Meet the Team

Mishal Fatima (Co-Founder & Barista Extraordinaire): When she's not sketching new latte-art designs, Mishal can be found sourcing single-origin beans from local roasters. Her warm smile and endless coffee-making stories are at the heart of our cafe culture.

Masooma Zahid (Co-Founder & Pastry Chef): Masooma's love of baking began at his grandmother's kitchen table.She blends traditional recipes with inventive flavors think saffron chiffon cake and lavender eclairs to give your taste buds a happy surprise.

Rishail Iman(Community Manager): Rishail organizes all of our events, from live music nights to charity bake sales. If you see her around, don't hesitate to say hello she's the friendly face behind every unforgettable Pink Cafe gathering.
